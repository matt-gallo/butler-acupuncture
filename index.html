Our Approach

We believe in treating the whole person, not just isolated symptoms.

Christopher Butler, L.Ac. combines the wisdom of traditional healing practices with modern functional medicine to create truly personalized care plans. Our approach focuses on:

  • Understanding your unique health history and goals
  • Identifying root causes through comprehensive assessment
  • Creating customized treatment strategies
  • Supporting your body's natural healing capacity
  • Empowering you with knowledge and tools for long-term wellness

Conditions We Treat

We treat chronic pain conditions including back pain, neck pain, joint pain, arthritis, fibromyalgia, and musculoskeletal disorders. Our integrative approach combines acupuncture with functional medicine to address both pain symptoms and underlying inflammatory processes, providing lasting relief without reliance on medication.

Specializing in IBS, SIBO, acid reflux, bloating, constipation, and other gastrointestinal issues. We identify root causes like gut dysbiosis, food sensitivities, and digestive enzyme deficiencies through comprehensive functional medicine testing, then create personalized treatment plans to restore optimal digestive function.

Expert care for thyroid disorders, adrenal dysfunction, menopause symptoms, PMS, PCOS, and other hormonal imbalances. Acupuncture helps regulate the endocrine system while functional medicine addresses nutritional deficiencies, stress factors, and metabolic issues affecting hormone production and balance.

Natural treatment for chronic stress, anxiety disorders, panic attacks, and emotional imbalance. Acupuncture regulates the nervous system and promotes relaxation, while functional medicine identifies physiological factors like blood sugar dysregulation, nutrient deficiencies, and neurotransmitter imbalances that contribute to anxiety.

Comprehensive treatment for insomnia, sleep disturbances, poor sleep quality, and circadian rhythm disorders. We address underlying causes including stress, hormonal imbalances, blood sugar issues, and neurotransmitter dysregulation through acupuncture and targeted functional medicine interventions.

Effective treatment for chronic fatigue, low energy, adrenal fatigue, and post-viral fatigue syndromes. Our approach investigates mitochondrial function, thyroid health, nutrient status, sleep quality, and stress response to identify and correct the root causes of persistent exhaustion.

Supportive care for autoimmune diseases including Hashimoto's thyroiditis, rheumatoid arthritis, lupus, and inflammatory conditions. We focus on reducing inflammation, supporting immune regulation, healing gut barrier function, and addressing environmental triggers through integrative medicine protocols.

Comprehensive metabolic health optimization including blood sugar regulation, insulin resistance, pre-diabetes, weight management, and cardiovascular risk reduction. Our functional medicine approach addresses diet, lifestyle, hormonal factors, and metabolic dysfunction to restore healthy metabolism.
